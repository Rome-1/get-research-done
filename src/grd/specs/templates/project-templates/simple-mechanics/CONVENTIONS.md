## Spacetime

### Coordinate Convention

**Value:** Cartesian 1D ($q \in \mathbb{R}$)

Generalized coordinate $q$ is the displacement from equilibrium.
Conjugate momentum $p = m \dot{q}$.

**Adopted:** Phase 1 (template)
**Justification:** Single degree of freedom; no need for curvilinear coordinates.
